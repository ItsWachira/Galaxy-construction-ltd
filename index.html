About the company

Galaxy Constructions LTD.

Founded in 2022, Galaxy Constructions Ltd has leveraged its extensive experience to develop a unique set of competitive advantages that allow it to successfully operate in the construction industry in Kenya. To date Galaxy Constructions has completed several building and infrastructure projects throughout Kenya for the private and public sector.

Our vision is to create long-term customer value via unrivaled service delivery by providing quality, efficient and reliable services.

Our mission is to become a trusted leading provider of high quality,safe, rapid and affordable construction and supplies services in Kenya.
